From a young age, it was clear that Eddie had a talent for absorbing his life and surroundings and regurgitating the particularly interesting bits through stories, songs and poems with his own brand of honesty, intellect, whit and east London tongue. He found fame within the indie pop band that he set up at college with his mates called 'loveable rogues' and has since been working on various solo project's exploring; spoken word, literature, acting, voice overs and of course his main love and purpose of delivering top notch lyrics over music.

Things in the pipe line: Eddie's main focus at the moment is getting his latest project 'mate' off the ground. This is a solo music project where Eddie has been working with DJ's to layer his poetry over beats. Amongst this in the background he has wrote a novel called 'Odd Herbert' and continues to chip away at the musical he is writing 'The tale of the Cockney Pirate'.
